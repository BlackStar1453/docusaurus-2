## Potential Issues and Solutions When Using Kimi and ChatGLM

When using Kimi and ChatGLM for the first time, you might encounter an issue with not being logged in. Click to open Kimi/ChatGLM, register, log in, and then restart GPT-Tutor to resolve this issue.
